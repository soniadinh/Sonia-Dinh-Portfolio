5 Body Senses

Interactive Design & Coding project. Students have to make 5 interactive prototypes using all 5 senses: Sight, Touch, and Taste. Here is an example of one of my student's project which combined all of these senses creating an augmented learning experience that teaches children about our different senses in a more immersive and playful way.

  • • Interactive study habits: sight, touch, and taste, form a narrative and make it funny
  • • The main goal is to be able to see how things work and how your hand perform certain tasks
  • • A way to see all the data together the process, the stages and understand the process
  • • How to collect data before you engage and make decisions base on prior knowledge
  • • To make a realistic simulation with knowledge of certain movements
  • • What sort of tools you need to complete the tasks
  • • What sort of knowledge you need
  • • What steps to be taken first
  • • How much budget you have
  • • Can't afford? DIY from raw materials
  • • What sort of time and estimated duration needed
  • • Challenges with discalcula, time blindness and sleeping inconsistent
  • • Need to identify the root cause
  • • Learn by connecting subjects through play-acting and simulation
  • • Create visual and auditory learning environments
  • • Make immersive experiences related to subject matter
